PUT YOUR PROJECT IMAGES HERE
============================

1. Export your work as JPG or PNG (roughly 1200–1600 px wide is enough).
2. Name files clearly, e.g. project-01.jpg, project-02.jpg
3. In index.html, change each <img src="..."> to point to your file.

Example:
  src="assets/images/my-video-still.jpg"

You can delete the placeholder-01.svg … placeholder-06.svg files once you use real images.
