Carla Honoré

Practice moves between light and chemistry, signal and sculpture, language and noise—films beside motion studies in TouchDesigner, tracks that behave like drawings, Blender volumes that borrow from the darkroom, and arguments with philosophy that settle on walls and screens. One slow document, written from Paris and elsewhere: how images circulate, and what they ask of us when they do.
